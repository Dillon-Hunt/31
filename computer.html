Objective

The objective of the game is to get a score of 31 with the same suit before your opponent (The Computer). Remember, it is posible to get 31 with 3 cards as long as the 4th is a different suit.

No Cards Left

In the case that the deck is depleated then both you and your opponent's heighest scores are calculated and whichever player is closest to 31 but not over is decleared the winner.

Tie

There is two senarios in which a tie is decleared. Firstly if both players start with 31, and secondly if the deck runs out and both players scores are the same.

Your Turn Stage 1

You start your turn by drawing (clicking) either a random card from the deck (the face down pile) or the top card of the discard pile (the face up pile).

2

Your Turn Stage 2

You end your turn by discarding (clicking) a card in your hand (the cards at the bottom of the screen) to return it to the discard pile. When you finish your turn your opponent will have it's turn. At the end of your turn you will always have 4 cards.

Calculating Your Score

To calculate your score you must first decide which suit you want to use, you will probbaly want to use the suit you have the most of. Then for each card of that suit add the value of the card. If you are unsure of the value of the card refer to this help menu.

Strategy

Now you can play, how can you win? My favorite strategy is to chose the suit that you have the most cards of and only collect cards of that suit. When you are drawing if a card of your suit is on the top of the discard pile you can use some addition to figure out what card is best to replace it with to get 31. Don't forget that you can pick up and discard the same card if it doesn't help you reach 31. Don't be afraid to go above 31, it is only dangerous if the deck has almost run out of cards.
