What is 31?

31 is a game card game in which the player and the computer are racing each other to get 31 first. 31 can be made of any cards as long as they are the same suit.

Why play 31?

31 is a fun card game that can help build mental arithmetic and exercise your brain. 31 is quick to learn, pickup and play, and the rounds don't go for to long. 31 is a fair game that does not encourage gambling like some other card games.

31 for education

The player is constantly calculating the score which builds their mental math skills and provides a fun alternative to worksheets. Teachers can use the built in game statistics to explain basic probability and ratios to younger students. The gameplay can improve problem solving skills and since it works in the browser, the students can play it on any device.

How to get started

To start playing 31 simply click “Sign Up” Then enter your name and username. When you first sign up you will be taken to the tutorial page so you can learn how to play.
