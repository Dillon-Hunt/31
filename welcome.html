What is 31?

31 is a game card game in which the player and the computer are racing each other to get 31 first. 31 can be made of any cards as long as they are the same suit.

Why play 31?

31 is a fun card game that can help build mental arithmetic and exercise your brain. 31 is quick to learn, pickup and play, and the rounds don't go for to long. 31 is a fair game that does not encourage gambling like some other card games.

How to get started

To start playing 31 simply click “Sign Up” Then enter your name and username. Once you have signed up you can play against the computer or another person with pass and play. To learn how to play click the help button when you are in the game.
