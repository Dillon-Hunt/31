First Turn

On your first turn you will be randomly dealt 4 cards. You will always go first so you will start your turn by drawing a card (by clicking) from either the discard pile (which is face up) or the deck (which is face down). After you draw you will have to discard one of your 5 cards in your hand. When you are discarding think about which card will bring your total to 31, this will be explained below. Look at the images below which depict what your first turn might look like.
